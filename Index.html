Artemis Spaceship Bridge Simulator
Up to 9 players 1-4 hours per mission

    Will you be:
  • Helm, deftly guiding your ship through all the dangers of the sector?
  • Weapons, locking on to your enemy before you send them to a fiery death?
  • Science, scanning for enemy weaknesses and providing navigational bearings?
  • Communications, coordinating allies and convincing foes that they should surrender while they still can?
  • Engineering, keeping systems running at peak efficiency while coordinating damage control teams to heal your ship?
  • Captain, taking input from the others to make the hard decisions that have to be made in order to survive and emerge victorious?
  • Smallcraft Pilot, flying your shuttle or fighter in defense of your ship?

The Artemis Spaceship Bridge Simulator lets you play any of these roles as you go up against fleets from multiple races who want to destroy your ship and your space stations in order to wrest control of the sector away from you. This is one of the most enjoyable team-building experiences that you will ever have!

Artemis Spaceship Bridge Simulator was created by Thom Robertson.
